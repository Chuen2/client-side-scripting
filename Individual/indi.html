Our campus

Universiti Malaysia Sabah (UMS, literally meaning, University of Malaysia of Sabah) is a public university in Malaysia. It was officially established on 24 November 1994 as the ninth public university in the country. The university is located on a 999-acre site at Sepanggar Bay in Kota Kinabalu in the Malaysian state of Sabah.
